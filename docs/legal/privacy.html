Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

1. Overview

Rebel Reality is designed to help UNLV students navigate campus buildings using augmented reality. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

To provide navigation services, the app may process:

  • Location data from your device (e.g., approximate indoor position)
  • Camera imagery for AR localization and overlays
  • Basic device information used for performance and debugging

3. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Render AR navigation arrows and guidance
  • Improve accuracy and reliability of navigation
  • Maintain app performance and troubleshoot issues

4. Data Storage and Sharing

Navigation-related data is processed in real time. We do not use it to personally identify you and do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Rebel Reality may rely on third-party services (such as mapping or AR platforms) to perform localization and rendering. These services are used only to support core navigation features.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be completely secure, and you use the application at your own risk.

7. Your Choices

You can disable location services or camera access in your device settings. Doing so may limit or disable AR navigation features within Rebel Reality.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@rebelreality.app
